Welcome to Amoxpohualistli, your personal speed reading trainer.

This application uses the RSVP method, which stands for Rapid Serial Visual Presentation.
With this technique, words are displayed one at a time at a fixed position on your screen.

This allows your eyes to stay focused on a single point rather than moving across the page.
The result is faster reading with less eye strain.

To get started, simply press the play button or press the Space key.
You can adjust the speed using the WPM controls or the arrow keys.

The highlighted letter helps your eye anchor to the optimal reading position.
Try different highlight positions to find what works best for you.

Happy reading!
